Solidarity Around the world


Though the main events are taking place in India, many NRIs (non-resident Indians) and other supporters are showing solidarity
by protesting, using social media, and other such methods. You can find out how to show solidarity even as an individual in a
different country by heading over to the How can you help page.



Canada


In Canada, NRIs and other supporters held car rallies, protested in front of the Indian consulate all across the country. Many even blocked highways and roads in places like Vancouver and Toronto.

United Kingdom


Same sorts of events played out in U.K. as it did in Canada, one big protest that occured there was the one held in front of the Indian High commision in London. Many MPs from an Indian, mostly Punjabi, origin called Dominic Raab (the foreign secretary) to raise awareness about the issue.

U.S.A


One comment that brought local farmers to the rallies against the 3 bills is when Joe Biden, the elected president, said "agriculture as usual". Protests in america are being held in many states with immense support

Korea


The solidarity protests in South Korea are spearheaded by the Korean peasent league. They started solidarity protests when they were sent a video explaining the details from their site in India. They and others started holding solidarity rallies.

Brazil


Though Brazil has not seen much solidaity protests, rallies, etc. "Brazil's Landless Workers Movement" has said it would support Indian farmers with Canada's organizations as well. In its letter, the organization acknowledges the government's plan, which the bills are a vital part of.

Italy


In Italy a shocking incident occured where pro-khalistani protestors vandalised the Indian embassy. Right before republic day in India, Khalistani protestors chanted "Khalistan Zindabad" (Long live Khalistan) and also carved it into many places in the embassy.
